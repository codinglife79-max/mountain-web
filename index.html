Hi, I'm Alex Trail.

Welcome to Alpine Tales. I created this space to share my passion for the highlands and the serenity found only above the treeline. Whether you are a seasoned climber or a weekend wanderer, there is a story here for you.

My journey began 10 years ago in the Rockies and has since taken me to the Andes, the Alps, and the Himalayas. This blog is a collection of guides, personal essays, and photography dedicated to the wild.
